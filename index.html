The problem we solve

Millions of smallholder farmers lack reliable access to clean water, while sitting under some of the highest solar irradiance on Earth. CGT turns that sunlight into a resource.

How it works

1

Capture

Contaminated or brackish water enters the still's evaporation basin.

2

Evaporate

Solar heat evaporates pure water, leaving salts and contaminants behind.

3

Collect

Vapour condenses on the cover and runs off as clean drinking water.
